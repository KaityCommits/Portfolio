About Me

Hi, I'm Kaitlyn! Software engineer, creative thinker, and artist.

I get inspired when I get to build, innovate, and bring ideas to life through technology. Whether it’s crafting sleek web experiences or streamlining processes, my goal is simple: help others through technology while making the digital world a more beautiful place.

When I’m not deep in lines of code, you’ll find me painting, experimenting with makeup, or expressing myself through fashion. Creating..whether on a canvas, a face, or a website has always been my happy place.
